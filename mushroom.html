French-Style Mushroom Stew

Description

Mushrooms simmered with onions, wine, and carrots make for a rich, French-style stew.

Serve with egg noodles, polenta, or mashed potatoes.

Ingredients

  • 16 ounces button mushrooms, chopped
  • 8 ounces cremini mushrooms, chopped
  • 8 ounces shiitake mushrooms, chopped

Steps

  1. Combine all four mushroom types in a large bowl with onions; toss gently to mix.
  2. Season mushroom-onion mixture with salt and pepper.
  3. Reduce heat to low and simmer, partly covered, until carrots and onions are tender and sauce has thickened, 30 to 40 minutes.
