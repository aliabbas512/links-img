Beef Tips and Tots

Description

I'm going to show you this very old-school method of making beef tips.

Which, once made, can be used to top lots of things—but my favorite thing to top with tips is probably Tater Tots.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ pounds beef round steak
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt, or to taste
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Taste beef tips and adjust the seasoning. Serve immediately over hot potato nuggets.
  2. Meanwhile, preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
  3. While meat simmers, bake potato nuggets in the preheated oven until crispy and heated through, 20 to 25 minutes.
